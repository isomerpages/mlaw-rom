<p><strong>1. Monetary Authority of Singapore's ("MAS") website - Lists of Designated Individuals and Entities</strong>
</p>
<p>MAS publishes &lt;a href="<a href="https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/anti-money-laundering/targeted-financial-sanctions/lists-of-designated-individuals-and-entities" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.mas.gov.sg/regulation/anti-money-laundering/targeted-financial-sanctions/lists-of-designated-individuals-and-entities</a>"
target="_blank"&gt;lists&lt;/a&gt; of individuals and entities designated
under the United Nations Act ("**UN Act**") and the Terrorism (Suppression
of Financing) Act ("**TSOFA**"). Licensed moneylenders should &lt;a href="
<a href="https://www.mas.gov.sg/subscription-services" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.mas.gov.sg/subscription-services</a>" target="_blank"&gt;subscribe&lt;/a&gt;
to the MAS mailing list to receive updated lists of designated individuals
and entities. Licensed moneylenders are required to screen their customers
against the lists before engaging in any business or commercial activity
with them.&lt;br&gt;&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p>You may refer to the &lt;a href="<a href="https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg/images/MAS%20Website%20Subscriber%20Services%20Guide_20191105_V1Final.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg/images/MAS%20Website%20Subscriber%20Services%20Guide_20191105_V1Final.pdf</a>"
target="_blank"&gt;MAS Website Subscriber Services Guide&lt;/a&gt; for
a step-by-step guide to the subscription process.&lt;br&gt;</p>
<p><strong>2. Inter-Ministry Committee on Terrorist Designation ("IMC-TD")</strong>
</p>
<p>The IMC-TD acts as Singapore's authority relating to the designation of
terrorists. This prohibits any person/ entity from dealing with or providing
any financial assistance to designated subjects, as required under the
TSOFA.</p>
<p>On MHA’s website on &lt;a href="<a href="https://www.mha.gov.sg/what-we-do/managing-security-threats/countering-the-financing-of-terrorism" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://www.mha.gov.sg/what-we-do/managing-security-threats/countering-the-financing-of-terrorism</a>"
target="_blank"&gt;IMC-TD&lt;/a&gt;, you will find information on counter
terrorism financing legislation, IMC-TD’s process of designation, and relevant
news articles.</p>
<p>To find out more about your obligations as a licensed moneylender on countering
terrorism financing, please click &lt;a href="<a href="https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg/images/Measures%20relating%20to%20prevention%20of%20terrorism%20financing.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://acd.mlaw.gov.sg/images/Measures%20relating%20to%20prevention%20of%20terrorism%20financing.pdf</a>"
target="_blank"&gt;here&lt;/a&gt;.</p>
